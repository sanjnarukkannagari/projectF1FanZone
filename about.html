F1 KNOWLEDGE

THE BASICS

Teams: Currently 10 teams with 2 drivers each.

Cars: Open-wheel, open-cockpit hybrid-powered race cars that can go over 200 MPH.

Races: About 20-24 races per season (called Grand Prix or GPs), held all around the world.

THE CHAMPIONSHIP

There are two championships:

  1. Drivers' Championship - for individual drivers
  2. Constructors' Championship - for teams (based on combined points of both drivers)

Points are awarded based on finishing position in each race.

The driver and team with the most points at the end of the season win the titles.

RECENT RACE UPDATES

Monaco GP 2025

Lando Norris won the 2025 Monaco Grand Prix, leading from start to finish and closing the championship gap to teammate Oscar Piastri. Charles Leclerc finished second, with Piastri third. Max Verstappen and Lewis Hamilton rounded out the top five. Despite new rules aimed at heightening the action, overtaking remained rare. McLaren now leads the Constructors' standings heading into the Spanish Grand Prix.
